About Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

Nuts and Bolts Puzzle is an intriguing logic game that challenges your spatial reasoning and problem-solving abilities. In this game, you're presented with a series of increasingly complex mechanical structures made up of nuts, bolts, and other mechanical components. Your task is to disassemble these structures piece by piece in the correct order.

What makes this puzzle unique is that you need to understand how mechanical components interact with each other. For example, you can't remove a nut if it's still securing a bolt, and you can't pull out a bolt if other pieces are blocking its path. This creates a satisfying chain of logical reasoning where you need to determine the precise sequence of disassembly.

With its realistic physics and attention to mechanical detail, Nuts and Bolts Puzzle offers a satisfying experience that mimics real-world engineering challenges. The game starts with simple structures but gradually introduces more complex mechanisms that will put your logical thinking to the test.

How to Play

  • Click on nuts, bolts, or other mechanical parts to interact with them
  • You must remove components in the correct logical order
  • Rotate and unscrew nuts by clicking and dragging in a circular motion
  • Pull out bolts by clicking and dragging in the direction they can move
  • Analyze the structure to determine which parts can be removed first
  • As you progress, the mechanical assemblies become more complex
