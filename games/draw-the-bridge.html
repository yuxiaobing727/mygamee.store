Draw the Bridge

Use your creativity to draw bridges that help characters cross dangerous gaps

About Draw the Bridge

Draw the Bridge is a creative puzzle game that challenges your ability to think outside the box. In this game, you'll use your drawing skills to create bridges, ramps, and paths to help characters safely cross dangerous gaps and obstacles. Each level presents a unique challenge that requires innovative solutions, making this game both entertaining and mentally stimulating.

How to Play

Use your mouse or touch screen to draw lines that will serve as bridges for the characters to cross. The key to success is understanding physics - your bridges need to be stable enough to support the weight of the characters as they move across. Some levels may require multiple bridges or specific shapes to overcome obstacles. Experiment with different drawing approaches to find the optimal solution for each challenge!

Tips and Strategies

  • Draw solid, connected lines to ensure stability
  • Consider the angle of your bridges - sometimes diagonal connections work better than straight lines
  • Use as few lines as possible to optimize your solution
  • Watch how characters move to understand the physics of the game
  • Don't be afraid to restart a level if your initial design doesn't work
