About Sparta Hoppers

Sparta Hoppers is an action-packed strategy game set in ancient Greece. Take command of a band of brave Spartan warriors and lead them to glory on the battlefield. The game combines simple yet addictive gameplay with tactical depth, challenging you to think strategically as you navigate your warriors across the battlefield.

Each level presents a unique battlefield layout with various obstacles and enemy positions. Your goal is to hop your Spartan warriors from one position to another, outmaneuvering enemies and capturing strategic points. The game becomes increasingly challenging as you progress, introducing new enemy types and more complex battlefield layouts.

With its simple controls but deep strategic elements, Sparta Hoppers offers an engaging experience for both casual gamers and strategy enthusiasts. Can you lead your Spartans to victory against overwhelming odds?

How to Play

  • Use your mouse to select a Spartan warrior
  • Click on a valid destination to move your warrior there
  • Plan your moves carefully - warriors can only hop to adjacent positions
  • Capture enemy positions by landing on them
  • Complete level objectives (such as eliminating all enemies or reaching a specific position)
  • Unlock new warrior types and abilities as you progress
