About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you fight against endless waves of monsters. Choose your character, each with unique abilities, and survive as long as possible while collecting power-ups and defeating increasingly challenging enemies.

How To Play

Use arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Your character attacks automatically when enemies are in range. Collect experience orbs to level up and choose new abilities or upgrades. The longer you survive, the stronger and more numerous the enemies become. How long can you last?

Game Features

  • Multiple playable characters with unique abilities
  • Dozens of different weapons and passive items to collect
  • Procedurally generated enemy waves for endless replayability
  • Challenging boss encounters
  • Skill-based progression system
  • Beautiful pixel art graphics and immersive soundtrack

Strategy Tips

Focus on movement and positioning to avoid getting surrounded by enemies. Prioritize upgrades that complement your character's strengths. Area-of-effect attacks are crucial for dealing with large groups of monsters. Don't forget to collect health pickups when available, and try to maintain a safe distance from dangerous enemies.
